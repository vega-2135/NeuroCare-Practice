What we treat and how we diagnose it

At NeuroCare Practice, we specialize in the diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of neurological conditions affecting the nervous system and muscles. Our skilled team prioritizes accurate diagnostics and personalized treatment plans. With a group of dedicated medical experts, we provide comprehensive expertise in diverse neurological areas, addressing issues such as headaches, sleep disorders, and various neurological diseases. Recognizing the individuality of each patient, we craft customized solutions to ensure the best possible outcomes. Your health and comfort are our primary focus, guiding our commitment to excellence in care.

Neurological conditions diagnosed and treated by us

  • Headaches
  • Cerebrovascular diseases
  • Epilepsies
  • Brain tumors
  • Dizziness and balance disorders
  • Back pain
  • Diseases of peripheral nerves
  • Movement disorders such as Parkinson's disease
  • Memory and concentration disorders
  • Muscle disease

Tests

  • Magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) of Head and Neck

    Image examination of of brain and neck organs.

  • Electroencephalogram (EEG)

    Recording of brain waves to detect possible disorders in the function of the brain.

  • Doppler sonography (cw-Doppler; Duplex, TCD)

    Ultrasound examination of vessels in the neck and brain.

  • Electromyography (EMG)/neurography (NLG)

    Examination of muscle currents (EMG) and nerve conduction velocities (NLG)

  • Cerebrospinal fluid diagnostics

    Sampling of the cerebrospinal fluid. Samples are taken from the lumbar spine

  • Blood tests
  • Memory tests
